About

I am a Robotics PhD candidate at the University of Utah advised by Dr. Tucker Hermans. I work at the LL4MA lab, where my research involves making robots with multi-fingered hands interact with objects (in-hand manipulation or dexterous manipulation). My robot buddies are the KUKA LBR4 robot and the Allegro Hand. More information here.
